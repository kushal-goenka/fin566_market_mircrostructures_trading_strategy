Detailed Description

Quote consists of a paired bid entry and an ask entry

This class is used as the base for quote data for market centers, quotes for market participants and quote summaries such as TopQuote (sum of liquidity from quotes at best prices), and NBBO quotes (the bid and offer from the 'best' exchange on each side, typically determined by something like a price, size, time priority rule)
